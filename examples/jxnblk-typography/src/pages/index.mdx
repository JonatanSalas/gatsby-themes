## Typography Demo

This section is meant to serve as a demonstration of what the typography styles look like in context.
Ideally, the base typographic styles will work well together when placed in UI boxes that use the styled-system spacing scale and appear generally centered. This should be tested with different base configurations in `@jxnblk/typography` and the conversion of Typography.js themes in `typography-system`.
